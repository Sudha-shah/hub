From being the first in my family to pursue higher education to becoming an award-winning author, my life is a testament to resilience and perseverance. At 73, I reflect on breaking societal norms, embracing lifelong learning, and advocating for women's empowerment. Join me as I share the stories and experiences that have shaped my path, celebrating the strength and significance of women in a male-dominated world.
